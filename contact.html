Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

When will CINEMADISE launch?

We're launching in 2025! Sign up for early access to be among the first to experience hassle-free cinema booking in Uganda.

Which cinemas are partnering with CINEMADISE?

We're currently in discussions with major cinema operators across Kampala. Partnership announcements will be made as we finalize agreements. Cinema operators interested in partnering can contact us at partners@cinemadise.ug.

What payment methods do you accept?

We support MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, and major credit/debit cards. All payments are processed securely through trusted payment gateways.

How does the seat selection work?

Our interactive seat map shows you exactly which seats are available in real-time. Simply click on your preferred seat(s), proceed to payment, and your seats are reserved instantly.

Can I book tickets for a group?

Absolutely! You can select multiple seats in one transaction, making it easy to book for your friends and family all at once.

I'm a cinema operator. How can I integrate CINEMADISE?

We offer flexible integration packages starting with our Starter tier at UGX 3 million. Contact our partnerships team at partners@cinemadise.ug to schedule a demo and discuss your specific needs.
