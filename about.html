Our Story

Transforming Uganda's cinema experience, one booking at a time

The Beginning

CINEMADISE was born from a simple frustration: standing in long queues at Kampala's cinemas, unsure if your preferred seats would still be available. As passionate film enthusiasts, we experienced this repeatedly and knew there had to be a better way.

Our journey began with research into Uganda's cinema landscape. We discovered that while the world had moved toward digital convenience, most of Uganda's movie lovers were still stuck with manual booking systems. Only one cinema chain offered online booking, while others relied on third-party solutions or no digital presence at all.

The Insight

Ugandans deserve the same seamless cinema experience enjoyed globally, but built specifically for our unique needs‚Äîmobile money integration, low-bandwidth optimization, and celebration of local cinema culture.

Inspired by global platforms like Fandango, but designed specifically for Uganda's unique needs, we set out to create something different. CINEMADISE isn't just about digitizing an old process‚Äî it's about building Uganda's cinema culture for the modern era.

We started with a prototype, pitched it to a cinema operator, and listened. We learned about their challenges with revenue tracking, demand prediction, and operational efficiency. We understood moviegoers' need for convenience, mobile money integration, and real-time information. From these insights, CINEMADISE was refined into the platform it is today.
